This is how we handle commercial construction project bids

  1. Identify a commercial project (department stores, malls, renovations, demolitions).
  2. Call seven subcontractors who can handle demolition, drywall framing, or painting in areas like New York, Rhode Island, Connecticut, New Jersey, and Pennsylvania, to name a few
  3. Collect quotes from subcontractors for the project.
  4. forward the quotes to my coworker, Jane, for administrative and technology handling.
  5. The CEO reviews the quotes and sends them to the head general contractor.
  6. Wait to see if the project is awarded to the client based on the submitted bid.
